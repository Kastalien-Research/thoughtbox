TARMED Billing Standard
  • Tariff types: 001 (TARMED), 311 (Physio), 316/317 (Lab), 400 (Drugs), 452 (MiGel)
  • Service records carry: code, ref_code, quantity, amount_mt (medical), amount_tt (technical)
  • Unit factors: unit_factor_mt/tt, scale/external factors
  • Obligation flag: determines patient vs insurance coverage
  • Billing roles: both/self_employed; medical roles
